**Foundation**:

Established in 1882, making it one of the oldest football clubs in England.

**Home Stadium**:

Plays at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, a world-class venue opened in 2019.

Tottenham Stadium

**Major Achievements**

Won two English league titles, eight FA Cups, and multiple European trophies.

**European Milestone**:

First British team to win a European trophy with their UEFA Cup Winners' Cup victory in 1963.

**Rivalries**:

Their fiercest competition is with Arsenal, culminating in the iconic North London Derby.
